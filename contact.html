Meet Karen

Hi, I’m Karen. I am a curious soul who is naturally drawn to problem solving and opportunities for learning and discovery. I strive to create elegant holistic solutions that speak to users and business goals on a deep level by leveraging qualitative and quantitative data.

During 2015- 2018, I joined F5 Networks as a software engineer. I was one of the two front-end developers in the Cloud Operating System Test team who is responsible on leading the design and implementation of multiple internal infrastructure tools to boost test case management and productivity. In addition to that, I also led the efforts to develop new process, design and standard across teams on internal infrastructure tools.

In May 2018, I joined Samsung Mobile as a UX designer, and my focus has been on crafting and fine-tuning user interactions and flows on mobile applications. Over the past year, I’ve designed and managed interactions and product solutions with North American carriers (AT&T, T-Mobile, Cricket Wireless, Tracfone) for Visual Voicemail, Call Protect, Mobile Hotspot, Mobile Tethering, Setup Wizard and more on all Samsung mobile devices.

When I’m not behind the screen, I consume ridiculous amount of audio and visual media on economics, design, politics, and food. My current favorite is the YouTube channel Cooking with Dog.

I am currently based in Seattle, but originally hailed from Hong Kong.

Thanks for stopping by.
