Meet Karen

Hi, I’m Karen. I am a curious soul who is naturally drawn to problem solving and opportunities for learning and discovery. I strive to create elegant holistic solutions that speak to users and business goals by leveraging qualitative and quantitative data.

During 2015- 2018, I worked at F5 Networks as a software engineer. I was one of the two front-end developers in the Cloud Operating System Test team who led the effort on design and implementation of multiple internal infrastructure tools across different teams.

In May 2018, I joined Samsung as a UX designer, and my focus has been on crafting and fine-tuning user interactions and flows on mobile applications. Over the past year, I’ve designed product solutions and managed design processes with internal cross functional teams and external mobile network operators (AT&T, T-Mobile, Cricket Wireless, Tracfone). Also, I was the UX project lead of multiple Samsung flagship devices(Galaxy S10, Galaxy Note 10, Galaxy Fold) and OS updates (Pie OS) for the North America market.

When I’m not behind the screen, I consume ridiculous amount of audio and visual media on economics, design, politics, and food. My current favorite is the YouTube channel Cooking with Dog.

I am currently based in Seattle, but originally hailed from Hong Kong.

Thanks for stopping by.
